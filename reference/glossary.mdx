---
title: "Glossary"
description: "Key terms and definitions used in Manteia."
---

### FlowNFT
An ERC-721 non-fungible token that represents a claim on a future revenue stream. It is the primary "asset" generated by the Manteia Protocol.

### MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
The predictable revenue a business expects every month. Manteia uses MRR volatility as a key input for risk scoring.

### ZK-SNARK
**Zero-Knowledge Succinct Non-Interactive Argument of Knowledge**. A cryptographic method that allows one party (the prover) to prove to another (the verifier) that a statement is true without revealing any information beyond the validity of the statement itself.

### The "Blind" Oracle
Manteia's architecture for ingesting off-chain data (from Stripe/Shopify) and verifying it on-chain without exposing the raw data payload.

### RWA (Real-World Asset)
Assets that exist off-chain but are tokenized and brought on-chain to be used as a source of yield or collateral.
